What is Social Media

Social media refers to websites and applications that allow people to create and share content, and to interact with each other online. It encompasses a wide range of platforms where users can connect, communicate, and engage with communities and content. These platforms facilitate the creation and exchange of user-generated content, fostering both personal and professional connections
